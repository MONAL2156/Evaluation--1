Hope For Change

Every contribution counts...

Hope for Change is a community-driven organization dedicated to empowering underserved communities through meaningful action. Our mission is to ensure equitable access to job opportunities and housing while actively contributing to the reimagining of community safety. We are committed to amplifying the voices of those most affected by systemic injustice. Our core values are centered around engaging with and supporting individuals directly impacted by societal inequities. We prioritize leadership development, political education, and leverage the insights and expertise of local community members to drive transformative change in their city and state. Through our initiatives, we aim to build collective power and foster a more just and inclusive society.
Our organization is black led and we focus deeply on hiring from our community and more specifically our base, we do this as we need to grow our movement to scale and we believe deeply that those who operate in our spaces should understand the dynamics of what our communities are experiencing on the day to day. Our organization is made up of black immigrants, black women, and folks who are affected by the carceral system both directly and indirectly this means we have a staff that see's our base as not just numbers but as folks who are experiencing the same things they have and may continue to regardless of their occupation
